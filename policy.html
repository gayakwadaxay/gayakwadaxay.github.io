Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 28, 2024

Introduction

Safegreet is committed to safeguarding the privacy of Visitors and Users of its Police and Hotel Management Software. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • From Visitors: Name, address, photo ID, selfie, and other personal details for verification and security.
  • From Users: Name, contact information, and login credentials for authorized personnel.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Verifying visitor identities against government databases.
  • Matching visitor selfies with criminal databases for safety purposes.
  • Enhancing visitor and user safety through proactive security measures.

3. Data Sharing

  • Law Enforcement Agencies: For security and criminal investigations.
  • Government Authorities: To authenticate photo IDs and identities.

We do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

  • Data is encrypted during transmission and at rest.
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel using secure authentication mechanisms.

5. Data Retention

  • Visitor data is retained for 10 years unless legally required for longer durations.
  • User data is retained for as long as the account remains active.

6. User and Visitor Rights

Visitors and Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data.
  • Request corrections to inaccuracies.
  • Request deletion of their data, subject to legal obligations.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@safegreet.in.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be communicated through the Software, and continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India, and any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Pune, Maharashtra.
